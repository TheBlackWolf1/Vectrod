📋 Photo Tips
💡
Good Lighting
Natural daylight or bright overhead lamp. No shadows across the letters.
📐
Shoot Flat
Hold phone straight above. Paper must be flat — table surface, not hand-held.
🖊️
Dark Pen, White Paper
Black or dark blue ink gives the cleanest detection. Avoid pencil if possible.
✂️
Write Each Letter Separate
Don't connect letters unless you want a cursive font. Leave small gaps between chars.
