How it works
✍️

Write a sentence that includes all the letters you want. Pen on white paper, big clear strokes.
📸
Photo It
Flat, well-lit shot. No shadows. Phone camera is fine. Landscape or portrait both work.
⬇️
Download Font
We detect each character, vectorize the strokes, build a real font. Download and install.
Photo tips for best results
💡
Good Lighting
Bright, even light. Natural daylight or lamp directly above. No shadows across letters.
🖊️
Dark Ink, White Paper
Black or dark blue pen. White or cream paper. High contrast = much better detection.
📐
Shoot Straight Down
Hold phone directly above paper — no angle. Paper perfectly flat, no curled edges.
🔤
Write Big & Clear
Larger than your normal handwriting. Leave space between letters — don't connect them.

Why Vectrod Handwriting Font
🔬
OpenCV Vectorization
We use adaptive thresholding + connected component analysis to isolate each character from your photo — even with uneven lighting or paper texture.
〰️
Smooth Bezier Curves
Raw pixel outlines become smooth cubic bezier paths. Your handwriting looks natural at any size — 8pt or 800pt.
📦
Real Font Format
Output is a real TTF + OTF with proper glyph metrics, kerning baseline, and Unicode mapping. Installs on any OS.
🔒
100% Private ✓
Your photo never leaves our server longer than needed. No storage, no account, no data collection. Processed and deleted.
⚡
No AI, No Guessing
We don't "synthesize" your handwriting — we extract your actual strokes from the photo. What you wrote is what you get.
🌐
Works Everywhere
TTF for Windows/Android, OTF for Mac/Adobe. Install in any app — Word, Figma, Illustrator, Canva, Google Docs.
