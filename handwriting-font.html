📋 Photo Tips for Best Results
💡
Good Lighting
Natural daylight or bright lamp. Avoid shadows across the page.
📐
Flat Photo
Hold phone directly above — no angle. Paper should be flat, not curved.
🖊️
Dark Pen
Black or dark blue ink on white paper. High contrast = best detection.
🔤
Write Clearly
Slightly bigger letters than normal. Don't connect letters (unless you want it cursive).
