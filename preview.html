How to Choose the Perfect Font for Your Project

Finding the right font is one of the most subjective ‚Äî and impactful ‚Äî decisions in any design project. The best way is always to see your actual content in the font, not just a generic sample. That's exactly what this tool lets you do.

Why "The Quick Brown Fox" Isn't Enough

Generic pangrams show all letters but don't reflect your real use case. A font might look beautiful in a sample but feel wrong with your brand name or tagline. Always test fonts with your actual words before committing.

Sans-Serif for Screens, Serif for Print (Mostly)

The old rule that serifs are for print and sans-serifs for screens has softened ‚Äî high-resolution displays render serifs beautifully. But sans-serifs like Inter, Poppins, and DM Sans remain dominant for UI because their clean strokes perform well at small sizes.

Display Fonts: Use Sparingly

Display fonts like Bebas Neue, Playfair Display, and Abril Fatface are designed for headlines and large sizes. They lose legibility ‚Äî and sometimes charm ‚Äî when set small. Use them for impact moments, not body copy.
